Our story

Started with one mission — honest gadget trade in Delhi

Sambhi Mobiles was started by Amrik Singh in Govind Puri, New Delhi. After seeing countless customers get underpaid at local shops or wait days for payment, we decided to build something better.

We come to your door. We pay instantly. We show every deduction upfront. No haggling, no spam calls, no hidden fees. Just the best price in Delhi — delivered at your doorstep.

Today we have completed over 2,400 transactions across all Delhi districts and maintain a 4.9-star customer rating. Every device is IMEI-verified and every transaction comes with a proper GST bill.
