Our Story

Built for Delhi. Built on trust.

In Delhi's secondhand phone market, customers were getting underpaid, deceived, and scammed daily. Retailers with no transparency, buyers who vanished after inspection, and a complete lack of documentation.

Amrik Singh started Sambhi Mobiles in 2021 with one mission: make selling your phone as simple and transparent as ordering food. No hidden charges. No haggling. Just a fair price, free pickup, and instant payment.

Today we've bought over 2,400 devices across Delhi NCR and built a reputation that speaks for itself — 4.9 stars across every platform.
