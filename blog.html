How to check IMEI before selling your phone in India

By Amrik Singh · Sambhi Mobiles · May 2025 · 4 min read

Every smartphone has a unique 15-digit IMEI number. In India, the government runs CEIR — Central Equipment Identity Register — a database of all stolen and blacklisted phones. Before selling any phone, you and the buyer should verify it.

How to find your IMEI number

  1. Open your phone dialer
  2. Dial *#06#
  3. Your 15-digit IMEI will appear on screen

Alternatively, go to Settings → General → About (iPhone) or Settings → About Phone (Android).

How to check if phone is stolen on CEIR

  1. Go to ceir.gov.in
  2. Click "Check IMEI Status"
  3. Enter your 15-digit IMEI
  4. Enter the captcha and click Check
✅ You can also check IMEI directly on our Sell page — free instant check powered by CEIR.

What does each result mean?

  • Clean / Not blacklisted — Safe to buy or sell. IMEI is not reported stolen.
  • Blacklisted / Blocked — This phone has been reported stolen or lost. Do not buy. The buyer will not receive cash from us for this device.
  • Unknown — Could not be verified. Check again or try at ceir.gov.in directly.
⚠️ At Sambhi Mobiles, we verify every IMEI before paying. Blacklisted phones will not be purchased — protect yourself by checking before we arrive.

Best time of year to sell your phone in Delhi — get 20% more

By Amrik Singh · Sambhi Mobiles · April 2025 · 3 min read

Most people sell their old phone whenever they feel like it — but timing makes a significant difference to how much you get. Based on 2,400+ transactions at Sambhi Mobiles, here is what we have found.

Best time to sell: September–October

Apple launches new iPhones every September. This creates the highest demand for old iPhones as buyers want a good deal on last year's model. September and October are the best months to sell your iPhone in Delhi — you can often get 10–15% more than at other times.

Good time to sell: January–February

After the Republic Day sales, many buyers look for pre-owned devices. There is high buying activity in January as people look for deals after the festive spending season.

Avoid: June–July

This is the slowest period for phone resale in Delhi. Demand is low, prices drop slightly. If you can wait, hold off until September.

Sell quickly after a new launch

The moment a new phone model is announced, the previous model's value drops by 8–12% within weeks. If you have an iPhone 15 and iPhone 16 just launched — sell your 15 within the first two weeks, not after.

iPhone vs Samsung resale value in Delhi — which holds value better?

By Amrik Singh · Sambhi Mobiles · March 2025 · 6 min read

This is the most common question we get: "Should I buy an iPhone or Samsung?" — and one of the best reasons to choose iPhone is resale value. Here is what our data from 500+ transactions shows.

After 1 year

  • iPhone 15 Pro (bought ₹1,34,900) → resale ₹85,000 — retains 63%
  • Galaxy S23 Ultra (bought ₹1,24,999) → resale ₹65,000 — retains 52%

After 2 years

  • iPhone 14 Pro (bought ₹1,29,900) → resale ₹55,000 — retains 42%
  • Galaxy S22 Ultra (bought ₹1,09,999) → resale ₹38,000 — retains 35%

After 3 years

  • iPhone 13 Pro (bought ₹1,19,900) → resale ₹40,000 — retains 33%
  • Galaxy S21 Ultra (bought ₹1,05,999) → resale ₹28,000 — retains 26%
📊 On average, iPhones retain 7–10% more of their value than equivalent Samsung phones after 2 years in Delhi's market.

Why do iPhones hold value better?

  • Apple provides iOS updates for 5–6 years, keeping older phones useful
  • Higher brand perception creates stronger demand in resale market
  • Consistent design language means older iPhones look less dated
  • Lower supply of second-hand iPhones (people hold them longer)

Does this mean you should only buy iPhone?

Not necessarily. Samsung flagships offer features iPhones do not — faster charging, better camera zoom, stylus support. If you use those features daily, the extra resale value may not outweigh the functionality you gain. The best phone is the one you will actually use.
